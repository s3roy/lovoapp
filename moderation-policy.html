Moderation Policy

Overview

This Interaction Policy outlines the usage of the chat feature, a free emotional wellness tool available on the Lovo platform (referred to as "Lovo").

This policy governs all interactions conducted through the chat feature. Any violations of this policy may result in account suspension or termination without refund or compensation.

Unmonitored Interactions

Communications through the chat feature are private and not actively moderated. Users are reminded of the importance of their emotional well-being and are encouraged to interact with kindness. Automated monitoring tools may be utilized for security purposes. Violations will lead to account suspension or termination without notice or compensation.

Disclaimer of Liability

Lovo is not responsible for the behavior or actions of users engaging with the chat feature. Any violations of the Interaction Policy, Terms of Service, or applicable laws will result in immediate action, including possible account suspension.

Legal Compliance

By using the chat feature, users confirm their legal authority to access and utilize it. Actions violating the rights of others, including privacy and intellectual property rights, are strictly prohibited.

Respectful Conduct

Users are expected to maintain civility in interactions. Abusive, offensive, or illegal behavior will not be tolerated and may result in immediate account suspension.

Privacy and Confidentiality

Users must not record, share, or reproduce conversations or other content exchanged through the chat feature. Any breach of this policy will result in immediate action.

Prohibited Content

Content involving harmful behavior, harassment, or any unlawful actions is strictly forbidden. Lovo utilizes automated tools to monitor and address such violations to ensure a safe space for emotional well-being.
