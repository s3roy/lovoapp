Community Guidelines

Lovo is a platform dedicated to emotional wellness and safe social interactions. The app currently offers a free chat feature that allows users to connect and support each other in a positive and respectful environment. To maintain this supportive atmosphere, we ask all users to adhere to the following Community Guidelines alongside our Terms of Use.

While using the app, please be kind and respectful to others, just as you would in a real-world interaction. Lovo is a community of people who come together to share experiences, seek support, and engage in uplifting conversations.

We urge you to avoid any actions that could lead to harm, distress, or emotional discomfort. As part of our commitment to emotional safety, we enforce a strict No Nudity Policy, and any identifiable nudity will result in content removal or termination of your session.

By following the guidelines below, we can ensure Lovo remains a platform where users can connect in a healthy, supportive way. Violations may result in account suspension, content removal, or other appropriate actions.

  • 1. Respectful Communication: Treat all users with kindness, empathy, and respect. Avoid using harmful language, personal attacks, or content that could cause emotional distress.
  • 2. Mental Health Support: While Lovo is a space for emotional support, we discourage promoting harmful behaviors. If you or someone you know is in crisis, please seek professional help or contact emergency services.
  • 3. Privacy and Safety: Always respect others' privacy. Do not share any personal information or images of others without their consent.
  • 4. No Hate Speech: Lovo is committed to maintaining a safe and inclusive space. Do not post any discriminatory or harmful content that targets individuals or groups based on race, gender, religion, or other protected characteristics.
  • 5. No Sexual Content: Lovo has a zero-tolerance policy for any form of sexual content, including pornography, inappropriate messages, or harassment.
  • 6. Mental Wellness Promotion: Content that encourages self-care, self-compassion, and positive mental health practices is always welcome. Please share resources that could benefit others in their emotional wellness journey.
  • 7. Cyberbullying: Lovo does not tolerate bullying, harassment, or malicious behavior. If you feel that you or another user is being harassed, please report it immediately.
  • 8. No Misinformation: Please ensure the accuracy of the information you share. Avoid spreading fake news or misinformation that could cause unnecessary panic or confusion.
  • 9. No Violence or Harmful Content: Do not share content that glorifies violence, self-harm, or suicide. If you believe someone is in danger, please contact a professional or a helpline immediately.
  • 10. No Scams or Fraud: Lovo is a platform for building genuine connections. Scams, phishing attempts, and fraudulent activities will not be tolerated.
